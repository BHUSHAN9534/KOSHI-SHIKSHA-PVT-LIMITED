To acquire accreditation or recognition for your company under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), ISO 9001:2015, and the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), you need to follow several specific procedures:

1. Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) – Company Act 2013:

To comply with the Companies Act of 2013, your company must be registered with the MCA. Since your company, Koshi Shiksha Private Limited, was established in 2024, it must already be incorporated under this act. Ensure compliance by:

  • Filing annual financial statements and returns.
  • Maintaining statutory registers and records.
  • Complying with provisions on board meetings, resolutions, and shareholder meetings.

If you need any further certification or filings, you can consult a legal or corporate advisor to ensure that your company adheres to the necessary corporate governance practices.

2. ISO 9001:2015 – Quality Management Systems:

ISO 9001:2015 certification demonstrates your company’s commitment to quality management. Here’s the process to get certified:

  • Gap Analysis: Compare your current operations with the ISO 9001:2015 standards to identify gaps.
  • Documentation: Prepare or update documents such as quality policies, objectives, procedures, and records.
  • Internal Audits: Conduct internal audits to ensure the system meets ISO standards.
  • Apply for Certification: Contact a certified body to conduct the external audit. After a successful audit, you’ll receive the ISO 9001:2015 certification.
  • Surveillance Audits: Post-certification, you will undergo periodic audits to maintain the certification.

You can approach certification bodies like TUV, SGS, or others for this process.

3. Ministry of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Registration:

MSME registration provides benefits such as subsidies, easier loan approvals, and government incentives. For this, your company needs to meet the criteria based on investment and turnover.

Registration Process:

  • Go to the Udyam Registration portal (udyamregistration.gov.in).
  • Enter your Aadhaar and PAN details.
  • Provide company information such as turnover and investments.
  • Submit the form to receive your MSME certificate.

Having these certifications will bolster your company's credibility and provide access to various benefits.

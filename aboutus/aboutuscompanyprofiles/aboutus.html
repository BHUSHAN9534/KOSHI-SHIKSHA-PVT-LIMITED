Koshi Shiksha Private Limited

About Us

Koshi Shiksha Private Limited, founded in March 2024, is a rapidly growing education company in India. Registered in New Delhi, it focuses on providing educational services and skill development programs, especially within the Bihar and Delhi regions.

Our Vision

The company aims to improve educational access and quality with the goal of making education universally accessible by 2030.

Services

We offer a range of services, including:

  • Tutoring
  • IT Training (e.g., web development and programming languages)
  • Consulting in finance, accounting, and GST compliance

Certifications & Initiatives

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, Koshi Shiksha aligns with several Indian government initiatives, such as Skill India and Startup India, showcasing its commitment to quality education and professional growth opportunities.
